GenCyber Summer Camp at RIT

What to Expect

July 15-19
2024 All Girls Summer GenCyber Camp

This is a day-long cybersecurity educational camp for students in the rising 8th - 12th grades, providing them with a comprehensive and immersive learning experience in cybersecurity.

What can participants expect?

  • A safe, inclusive learning enviornment.
  • The day will be a mix of instruction and experimental hands on activities.
  • A recreational break and lunch is provided.
  • No cost for participants.
  • The camp will run from Monday to Friday.

What we expect from the participant:

  • No experience in computing or cybersecurity is required.
  • Good attitude and willingness to learn!

What this camp is not!

  • This camp is not a replacement for childcare. This camp is for participants who want to learn more about computing and cybersecurity. Participants who clearly demonstrate a lack of interest in attendance and learning in an inclusive enviornment will not be asked to return for remaining sessions.

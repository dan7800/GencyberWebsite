Camp Info

2019-2020 Summer Camps

There will be TWO opportunities to participate in GenCyber at RIT this summer. We will offer a one-week coed camp and a one-week camp for girls. Both weeks will follow the same curriculum. GenCyber will be offered in collaboration with RIT's Camp Tiger on the RIT campus with RIT faculty, staff, and students serving as instructors. For more information on Camp Tiger please visit the Camp Tiger website.

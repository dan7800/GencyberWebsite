Camp Info

2019-2020 Summer Camps

The will be TWO opportunities to participate in GenCyber at RIT this summer. We will offer a one-week coed camp and a one-week camp for girls. Both weeks will follow the same curriculum. GenCyber will be offered in collaboration with RIT's Camp Tiger on the RIT campus with RIT faculty, staff, and students serving as instructors. For more information on Camp Tiger please visit the Camp Tiger website.

Co-Ed

July 8-12, 2020

Our co-ed camp will be held July 8-12, 2020. This camp welcomes all students who want to learn more about cyber security, cyber ethics, online safety, cyber security and defense.

Girls

July 22-26, 2020

Our girls camp will be held July 22-26, 2020. This camp welcomes all girls who want to learn more about cyber security, cyber ethics, online safety, cyber security and defense.
